About Website

"Thank you for visiting our website! We are a group of BS Computer Science students with a shared passion for Filipino cuisine, love for places, and technology. As part of our project, we aim to explore the rich and diverse flavors of Filipino food, from savory classics like Adobo to delightful desserts like Halo-Halo, while showcasing the breathtaking beauty of famous Filipino destinations".

"Although our primary focus is technology, we have thoroughly enjoyed immersing ourselves in the unique flavors and traditions that make Filipino food so special. Our goal is to introduce you to some of the most beloved Filipino dishes and iconic locations, inspiring you to experience them for yourself".

"This website was created using HTML and CSS. Through this project, we have learned how to bring together design, content, and functionality with the use of HTML and CSS".

"We decided to name our website tampokLokal because it highlights the richness of Filipino cuisine, and destinations. 'Tampok' means to highlight, and 'Lokal' emphasizes our local treasures, perfectly capturing our mission to showcase the unique aspects of the Philippines".

"Thank you for checking out our project! We hope you enjoy discovering the wonders of Filipino cuisine and places with us".
